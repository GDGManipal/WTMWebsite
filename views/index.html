    About Us

    Women Techmakers, in association with Google Developers Group is aimed at increasing woman participation in all spheres of Computer Science and Technology.It's Manipal chapter, Code Czarinas, created with the above objective, combines technology with team spirit and hard work. At Manipal, our community aims at progressive, community-based learning via long term projects. The weekly meet-ups, workshops,talks by speakers and group discussions are vital to strengthening one's technical skill-set, gaining exposure to the plethora of technological domains and building connections.
